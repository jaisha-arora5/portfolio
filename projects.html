JIIT Marauder's Map

A magical integration of Harry Potter's nostalgia with real life JIIT campus map. Users signs up with their accounts to initiate their entry. Further, they can explore and join rooms and even club with friends to track their glowing footsteps which reveals their current location on the beautiful vintage styled map. Thoughtfully, one may find their friends roaming in the campus grounds on late night strolls and meet them hassle-free.
